Cafe Coffee

Welcome to Cafe Coffee Bray, we are a local coffee shop with a team of experienced, skilled baristas. Whether it is coffee, tea or hot chocolate we provide the best in Bray.

Our Promise

All of our tea and coffee is sourced from sustainable plantations across the globe including in Brazil, colombia and India.

Experienced Team

We are an experienced team of baristas all of whom have over 5 years experience in the coffee shop industry. We all strive to give you the best experience possible, through high quality products and friendly communication.

Menu

Cafe Coffee offer a large range of products for both in shop and take away. We offer a selection of tea, coffee, hot chocolate and cold drinks and snacks to go with them.

About Us

We are an independant coffee shop lacated in Bray, Co.Wicklow. We are Bray owned and staffed and aim to provide the best coffee shop experience in town. We believe, our large menu filled with high-quality products created by caring, skilled baristas combined with the friendly atmosphere in our Main Street store create the perfect coffee shop for us and you.
